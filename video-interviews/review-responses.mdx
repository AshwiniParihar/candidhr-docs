## Overview

Once candidates complete their video interviews, you can review their recorded responses directly in the platform. AI scoring provides an immediate performance summary before you watch the videos.

---

## Reviewing a Candidate

1. Open the **Automated Video Interview** section from the left navigation
2. Find the candidate with a **Completed** status
3. Click on the candidate to open their response page
4. Review the **AI Score** for an initial performance overview
5. Watch each recorded response to evaluate in detail

---

## AI Score

The AI score provides an immediate performance preview based on the candidate's recorded responses. It covers:

- Clarity and communication
- Response relevance
- Confidence and delivery

<Note>
  AI-generated scores are intended to support recruiter decision-making and should not be the sole basis for candidate evaluation. Always watch the full responses before making a decision.
</Note>

---

## Best Practices

- Use AI Scores as an initial filter to prioritise which candidates to review first
- Always watch the complete video responses before making a hiring decision
- Compare responses across candidates for the same role to ensure consistent evaluation
- Document your observations using notes to support collaborative hiring decisions
