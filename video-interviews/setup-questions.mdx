## Overview

Build reusable Question Banks to ensure consistency and fairness across similar roles. Question banks can be shared with teammates and reused across multiple interviews.

## Creating a Question Bank

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new bank">
    Click **+ Create** to start a new question bank. Name it clearly so teammates can identify it (e.g. "Senior Sales Role").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add questions">
    For each question, set a time limit between **30 and 600 seconds**.

    - **30-60 seconds** works for quick factual questions
    - **2-3 minutes** is better for situational questions like "Tell me about a time when..."
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Changes made to shared question banks may impact future interviews that use those questions.
</Warning>

---

## Question Types

| Type | Best For |
|---|---|
| **Technical Questions** | Assess role-specific knowledge and skills |
| **Behavioural Questions** | Evaluate past experiences and situational responses |
| **Communication Questions** | Gauge clarity, confidence, and articulation |

---

## Best Practices

- Create separate question banks for different job roles and departments
- Use clear and standardised naming conventions for easier collaboration
- Keep questions role-specific and aligned with actual job responsibilities
- Maintain a balanced interview structure using both technical and behavioural questions
- Periodically review and update question banks to match evolving hiring requirements
