## Overview

Bulk actions let you act on multiple applicants simultaneously, saving time when managing large volumes of candidates.

---

## Selecting Applicants

Check the **checkbox** on the left of each row in the Applicants table. You can select individual rows or use the **header checkbox** to select all applicants on the current page.

Once at least one applicant is selected, the **Bulk Action Bar** appears at the bottom of the screen.

---

## Update Status

Change the application status for all selected candidates at once.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select applicants">
    Check the rows of the candidates you want to update.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Update Status">
    Click **Update Status** in the Bulk Action Bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new status">
    Select the new status (e.g. Rejected, On Hold, Shortlisted).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify candidates (optional)">
    Toggle **Email candidates** to send a status update to all selected applicants. Customise the subject and message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Update** to apply the change to all selected applicants.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Bulk status changes apply immediately to all selected applicants. Double-check your selection before confirming, especially when bulk rejecting candidates.
</Warning>

---

## Export CSV

Export the filtered or selected applicant list as a CSV file.

1. Apply any filters to narrow your list
2. Optionally select specific rows, or leave all unchecked to export the entire filtered set
3. Click **Export CSV** in the filter bar

The export respects active filters and sort order.

---

## Clear Selection

Click **Clear** in the Bulk Action Bar to deselect all checked applicants.

---

## Best Practices

- Filter the list first before selecting, to avoid accidentally including unintended candidates
- When bulk rejecting, enable email notifications so candidates receive a timely response
- Use Export CSV after filtering by job or status for targeted reporting
