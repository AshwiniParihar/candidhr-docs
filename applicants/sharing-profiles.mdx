## Overview

Sharing a candidate profile grants specific team members access to view that applicant's full profile - including their resume, stage results, and AI scores - even if they do not have access to the job by default.

This is useful for bringing in hiring managers, subject matter experts, or interviewers on a per-candidate basis without giving them broad access to all jobs.

<Note>
  Sharing requires the SHARE_APPLICATION permission. Contact your Admin if you do not see the share option.
</Note>

---

## Sharing a Single Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the applicant profile">
    Click the candidate's name from the Applicants list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Share">
    Click the **Share** button in the profile header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for team members">
    Use the search field to find team members by name or email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add members">
    Select the users to add. They appear in the shared users list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove access">
    To revoke access, click the remove icon next to a user's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Confirm your changes. Selected users now have access to this profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>



---

## Best Practices

- Share profiles with interviewers before their sessions so they can review the candidate's resume and stage history in advance
- Revoke access after the hiring decision is made to keep profile access tidy
