## Overview

The Resume Screening module lets you review, analyse, and manage candidate resumes using AI-generated scores, status tracking, and application management tools.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Resume Screening">
    Click **Resume Screening** from the left-side navigation panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse candidates">
    The Resume Screening page displays all candidate information. Use filters and search to locate specific applicants.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How AI Scoring Works

When resumes are received, the system automatically assigns a **resume score** based on predefined evaluation parameters. The score reflects how well a candidate's resume matches the job's configured criteria including skills, experience, education, projects, and certifications.

Resume scoring weights are configured per job during the job creation process. See [Scoring & Preferences](/resume-screening/scoring-and-preferences) for details.

**Best Practices**
- Regularly review high-scoring resumes for faster shortlisting
- Keep job details and required skills updated to improve AI scoring accuracy
- Use filters to segment candidates by score range or status
