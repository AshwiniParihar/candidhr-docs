## Overview

The Settings section lets you manage everything related to your account, organization, and team. Access it from the **bottom-left corner** of the platform by clicking your profile icon or username.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings/image2.png" alt="Accessing the profile section from bottom-left navigation" />
</Frame>

The first user created during onboarding is always assigned the **Admin** role and is responsible for:

- Creating new users
- Assigning roles and permissions
- Managing organization settings
- Controlling team access

<Warning>
  Always sign out after completing your work, especially when using shared or public computers. The **Sign Out** option is available from the profile menu.
</Warning>

## Account Info

Manage your personal profile details including name, photo, contact information, and role.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings/image5.png" alt="Account Info tab showing profile details" />
</Frame>

### Photo Section

1. Click the upload area or icon
2. Select a professional, clearly cropped image from your device
3. Click **Update** to save the change

### Basics

| Field | Purpose | How to Update |
|---|---|---|
| Full Name | Identifies you across the platform | Click **Edit** next to the field and save |
| Email | Used for login and notifications | Contact your administrator |
| Phone Number | Account communication and verification | Click **Edit** and enter the number |
| Country | Personalises regional settings and workflows | Click **Edit** and select the country |
| State | Provides accurate location information | Click **Edit** and enter the state |
| Job Title | Identifies your professional role | Click **Edit** and enter the title |
| Role | Defines what actions you can perform | Managed by your administrator |

<Note>
  Some profile settings and management options may only be accessible to Admin users.
</Note>

**Best Practices**
- Keep personal profile details updated for accurate communication
- Use a professional profile picture for better collaboration
- Review role permissions periodically
- Update contact details whenever changes occur

## Organisation Info

Manage your company profile, branding, contact details, and social presence. This information appears across candidate-facing communications, job invitations, and reports.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings/image6.png" alt="Organisation Info settings page" />
</Frame>

### Organization Logo

| Setting | Details |
|---|---|
| Supported Formats | SVG, PNG, JPG |
| Recommended Size | 256 × 256 pixels, max 2 MB |
| Appears In | Candidate emails, job invitations, candidate-facing pages, reports |

Click **Replace Logo**, select your image, and save.

<Tip>
  Use a high-quality logo with a transparent background (PNG or SVG preferred) for consistent branding across all recruitment materials.
</Tip>

### Contact & Support

| Field | Example | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Support Email | support@company.com | Candidate support and system notifications |
| Contact Email | contact@company.com | Official business communication channel |
| Contact Phone | +91 XXXXX XXXXX | Direct contact for candidates and partners |

### Web & Social

| Field | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Organization Website | Recommended | Appears in invitations, reports, and communications |
| LinkedIn | Recommended | Improves professional visibility and employer trust |
| Facebook | Optional | Share company culture, events, and announcements |
| Twitter / X | Optional | Useful for active recruitment campaigns |

### Location & Size

| Field | Purpose | Example |
|---|---|---|
| State | Regional categorisation and reporting | Maharashtra |
| Country | Country-based reporting and localisation | India |
| Organization Size | Analytics, benchmarking, and insights | 500+ employees |

**Best Practices**
- Use official company branding assets such as logos and domain names
- Maintain updated organisation details for accurate candidate communication
- Use monitored support and contact email addresses
- Keep social media links and company descriptions current and professional
