## Before you begin

Make sure you have:
- A CandidHR recruiter account
- Your organisation set up (Settings → Organisation Info)
- At least one team member with the appropriate role

---

## Step 1 — Create a job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Jobs">
    Click **Jobs** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Job">
    Click **+ New Job** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Enter the job title, department, location, and description. Click **Next**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set preferences">
    Choose the skills, experience range, and education requirements you want to screen for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a workflow">
    Select a pre-built workflow or create a new one. The workflow defines the stages candidates move through.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Click **Launch Job**. Your job is now live and the public application link is ready to share.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 2 — Share the job link

After launching, copy the **Public Job Link** from the job overview page and share it via:
- Your company careers page
- LinkedIn, Naukri, or other job boards
- Direct email to candidates

---

## Step 3 — Review your first applicant

Once applications come in:

1. Go to **Applicants** in the sidebar
2. Click any applicant row to open their profile
3. Use the **Overview** tab to see their personal and professional info
4. Check the **Resume** tab for parsed resume data and screening score

<Tip>
  Use the **Bulk Actions** menu to move multiple candidates to the next stage at once.
</Tip>
