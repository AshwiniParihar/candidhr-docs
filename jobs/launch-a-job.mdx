## Before launching

Make sure your job has:
- [ ] A job title and description
- [ ] Screening preferences configured (skills, experience, education)
- [ ] A workflow assigned
- [ ] At least one team member with recruiter access

<Note>
  You can launch a job without screening preferences, but resume scoring will not be available.
</Note>

---

## Launching the job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the job">
    Go to **Jobs** and click the job you want to launch.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review settings">
    On the job overview page, check the **Details**, **Preferences**, and **Workflow** tabs to confirm everything is correct.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Launch">
    Click the **Launch Job** button in the top-right corner of the job overview.

    A confirmation dialog will appear — click **Confirm Launch**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the application link">
    Once launched, the **Public Application Link** appears at the top of the job overview. It looks like:

    ```
    https://app.candidhr.com/apply/JOB-KEY-HERE
    ```

    Copy and share this link on:
    - Your company's careers page
    - LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, or other job boards
    - Direct email outreach to candidates
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## What happens after launch

When a candidate visits the application link:
1. They see the **public job detail page** with the title, description, and requirements
2. They click **Apply Now** and verify their email via OTP
3. They complete the multi-step application form (personal info → professional info → custom questions)
4. Their application is submitted and appears in your **Applicants** list
5. If you have a workflow, they automatically enter the first stage

## Managing job status

### Close a job

Closing a job stops new applications. Existing applicants remain and can still be processed.

1. Open the job
2. Click **...** (more options) → **Close Job**
3. Confirm

### Reopen a job

A closed job can be reopened at any time. The same application link becomes active again.

1. Open the closed job
2. Click **Reopen Job**

### Archive a job

Archiving hides the job from your default jobs list. All data is preserved.

1. Open the job
2. Click **...** → **Archive Job**

To view archived jobs, toggle **Show Archived** in the jobs list filters.

## Sharing via Deep Search (Scout)

You can also invite specific candidates directly without making the job fully public:

1. Open the job → **Scouted** tab
2. Click **Scout Candidate**
3. Enter the candidate's name and email
4. They receive a personalised invitation link

See [Deep Search](/applicants/sharing-profiles) for more details.
