## What is a Job in CandidHR?

A **Job** is the central object in CandidHR. It represents an open position and contains:

- Job details (title, description, location, department)
- Screening preferences (skills, experience, education)
- A **Workflow** — the stages candidates go through (resume screening → assessment → video interview → interview)
- Applicant data — all candidates who applied to this specific role

---

## Job States

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Draft** | Created but not yet live. Candidates cannot apply. |
| **Active** | Live. The public link is accessible and accepts applications. |
| **Closed** | No longer accepting applications. Existing applicants remain visible. |
| **Archived** | Hidden from the default jobs list. Data is preserved. |

---

## Jobs List View

The Jobs page (`/app/user/jobs/v2/`) shows all your jobs in a table with:

- Job title and department
- Number of applicants
- Status badge
- Workflow assigned
- Date created and launched

You can filter by status, sort by date or applicant count, and search by title.
