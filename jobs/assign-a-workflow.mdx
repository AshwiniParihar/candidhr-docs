## Overview

Assigning a workflow to a job activates the automated hiring pipeline for that job. All new applicants enter the workflow automatically based on the invite options you configure.

## How to Assign a Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Job">
    Go to **Jobs** from the left sidebar and click the job you want to configure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Workflow tab">
    Inside the job details, click the **Workflow** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Assign Workflow">
    Click the **Assign Workflow** button. The assignment modal opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a workflow">
    Search for and select the workflow from the dropdown. You can type to filter by name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose invite options">
    Select at least one invite option (see below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Assign Workflow** to activate. Candidates will now enter the workflow automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Invite Options

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Include from job application form** | Candidates who apply through the job's application form are automatically added to the workflow |
| **Invite via email** | Manually enter email addresses to invite specific candidates directly |

You can enable both options at the same time.

### Inviting via Email

When **Invite via email** is selected:

1. Type an email address into the input field
2. Press **Enter** or **Space** to add it as a chip
3. Repeat for additional addresses
4. Click the **X** on any chip to remove it

<Note>
  To see candidates who were previously invited by email, toggle **View existing emails** in the modal.
</Note>

---

## Configuration Warnings

If the selected workflow has stages that are not fully configured, an amber warning banner appears showing which stage needs attention.

You must resolve all configuration issues before the **Assign Workflow** button becomes active.

---

## Changing or Removing a Workflow

To replace or remove a workflow from a job, go to the **Workflow** tab inside the job and click **Configure Workflow**. This reopens the assignment modal where you can select a different workflow.

<Warning>
  Changing a workflow on a job only affects new candidates. Candidates already in the previous workflow continue on their existing version.
</Warning>

---

## Permissions

The **Assign Workflow** action requires the `ASSIGN_WORKFLOW` permission. Contact your Admin if you do not see this option.
